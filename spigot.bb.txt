[SIZE=5][B][COLOR=#FF0000]Placeholders:[/COLOR][/B][/SIZE]
Surround the placeholders with %% for PlaceholderAPI and {} for MVdWPlaceholderAPI.
Example: %example% and {example}
[B]cooldownsx_ender_pearl[/B]: Display the ender pearl cooldown
[B]cooldownsx_golden_apple[/B]: Display the golden apple cooldown
